1. Introduction

Welcome to Granvela ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our storefront or sign in using Login with Amazon ("LWA"). By accessing or using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Category Examples Source
Identity Data Name, Amazon user ID Provided by Amazon via LWA
Contact Data Email address, default shipping address (if you grant permission) Provided by Amazon via LWA

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as social security numbers or health data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Fulfil Orders — process payments, arrange shipping, and provide order confirmations.
  • Provide Customer Support — answer enquiries, handle returns, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improve Services — analyse aggregate usage to enhance site performance and product offerings.
  • Send Marketing Communications — deliver newsletters or promotions only if you have opted in; you may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Comply With Legal Obligations — satisfy tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, and regulatory requirements.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We share information only with:

  • Service Providers — payment processors, logistics partners, IT hosting, and analytics vendors bound by confidentiality.
  • Amazon — for authentication, order fulfilment, and reporting under the Amazon Services Business Solutions Agreement.
  • Public Authorities — when required to comply with subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes.

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use first- and third-party cookies, web beacons, and local storage to:

  • remember cart contents and login state;
  • analyse site traffic and performance;
  • personalise content and ads (where permitted).

You can disable cookies in your browser; however, some features may not function properly.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • fulfil the purposes described in this Notice;
  • satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting obligations;
  • resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it.

7. Security

We protect your information using administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including:

  • TLS encryption in transit;
  • hashed and salted credentials in storage;
  • role-based access controls and regular security audits.

Despite our efforts, no internet transmission or storage system is 100 % secure; you use our services at your own risk.

8. Your Choices & Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • request access to the personal data we hold about you;
  • correct or update inaccurate data;
  • delete your data or restrict its processing;
  • opt out of marketing communications;
  • receive a portable copy of your data;
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.
